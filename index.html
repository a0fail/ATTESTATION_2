Nyuta Federmesser

The woman who helped many and will help more

Photo by Buro.

Here's a time line of Nyuta Federmesser's life:

  • 1977 - born in Moscow
  • 1994 - Nyuta graduated with honours from Moscow school No.1507 and started volunteering in hospitals
  • 1995—1997 - she studied English in Cambridge
  • 2000 - graduated from The Maurice Thorez Institute of Foreign Languages with specialty of theatrical simultaneous interpreter
  • 2006 - Nyuta founded 'Vera' endowment fund, the first of its kind in Russia
  • 2013 - graduated from I.M. Sechenov First Moscow State Medical University with specialty of "Health Organization"
  • 2016 - became a head of Center of palliative help of Moscow Health Department
  • 2019 - co-founded the 'Dom c Mayakom' ('A Lighthouse') in-patient care facility for children with oncology
